About Us

Achieving Your Corporate Goals with Personalized Events. At Cityneon® we focus on creating unique experiences for our clients. Our expertise lies in planning and executing events that meet clients’ objectives while exceeding their expectations. Cityneon® is comprised of a team of skilled planners who work closely with clients to understand their vision and goals for the event. One of the key strengths of Cityneon® is the ability to customize events to meet the unique needs of clients. Whether they’re planning a corporate conference, a public event, or any other, we have the expertise to create an event that is tailored to their specific needs and budget. Cityneon® is known for its exceptional customer service and attention to detail. Overall, Cityneon® is a trusted partner for clients who want to create unforgettable experiences. We have the expertise, creativity, and passion to bring any event to life, and our clients know that they can rely on us to deliver exceptional results every time.

Founder & Chairman's Message

Our family believes that continuity and growth are achieved by building on a solid foundation. We have established our principles and values as the pillars of our operations. We believe in the potential development of people around us and the prosperity of communities if given the right opportunity. Thus, our core principles focus on investing in projects that directly benefit people and society. This is the measure by which we gauge the success and importance of any investment we partake in, which is why healthcare, education, clean energy, and artificial intelligence are at the forefront of our investment activities. We take pride in our investment in Alnahdi Medical Company, alongside our other achievements. We are committed to our partnerships and responsibilities towards our investment projects and partners. Our success constantly reminds us of the importance of humility, and we view every failure as an opportunity to learn and grow. We aspire to expand our positive impact as far as possible by spreading our values and translating them into all our work, looking forward to passing on this responsibility and success to future qualified generations who will continue the message.
